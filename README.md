The system learns your natural rhythm and suggests when to fan the flames or let embers cool.

## 🌌 Overview

Gryffin Forge is a **next-generation orchestration layer** for knowledge workers, creative teams, and solo entrepreneurs who refuse to compromise between structure and fluidity. In 2026, the digital landscape demands tools that adapt to human behavior—not force humans to adapt to rigid tooling.

Our platform integrates **reactive task visualization**, **semantic clustering**, and **temporal energy mapping** to create a workspace that breathes with you. Whether you are plotting a novel, managing a product launch, or organizing a community movement, Gryffin Forge adjusts its interface and logic to match your unique cognitive fingerprint.

> *"The forge becomes part of the blacksmith."*

We replace the static checklist with a living canvas. Instead of linear "to-do" lists, you get **"Thermal Cascades"** —sequences where each completed task releases heat energy that powers the next phase. Suddenly, motivation is not abstract—it is measured in **calories of creativity**.

## ✨ Key Features

### 🧠 Intelligent Semantic Clustering
Forget manual tagging. Gryffin Forge parses your notes, tasks, and documents using a lightweight on-device model (no data leaves your machine) and groups them by conceptual heat—related ideas gather into "constellations" that you can name, collapse, or expand. This turns a flat project board into a **3D knowledge nebula**.

### ⚡ Thermal Momentum Tracking
Every action you complete generates "momentum points." When you hit a streak of five completed tasks, the interface glows gently, signaling peak flow state. Momentum decays if you stay idle for too long, but the system offers **"re-ignition prompts"** —small, low-friction tasks to warm you back up.

### 🎨 Adaptive UI with ChromaShift™
The interface learns your favorite color palettes, font sizes, and even preferred text density. Over time, ChromaShift adjusts contrast, background warmth, and element spacing to reduce eye strain and mental fatigue. It’s like a pair of glasses for your workflow.

### 🔄 Multi-Platform Synchronization
Work on your desktop, continue on your tablet, and review on your phone—all without losing context. The **Thermal Engine** syncs only the delta changes (like a breathing pattern), ensuring minimal data usage and near-instant reflection. No accounts needed for sync—uses peer-to-peer encrypted mesh.

### 📊 Visual Heatmaps & Reports
At the end of a sprint or month, Gryffin Forge generates a **Thermal Retrospective**—a beautiful, heatmap-style report showing where your energy went, how long tasks stayed "hot," and suggestions for future workflow optimization. It’s like a fitness tracker for your productivity.

## 🏗 Architecture & Design

Gryffin Forge is built on a **micro-frontend, event-driven architecture** that prioritizes user sovereignty:

- **Client-First Logic**: The core runtime runs entirely in your browser or local device. No external servers are required for daily operation.
- **Offline Resilience**: The forge never goes cold. All features (except collaborative sync) work completely offline with full data integrity.
- **Minimal Dependencies**: Less than 5% of the codebase relies on third-party libraries, reducing attack surface and bloat.
- **Web Components for UI**: Every interface element is a custom web component, making them lightweight, accessible, and styleable via CSS variables.

The data store uses a **content-addressable graph database** where each node is a "nugget" (idea, task, note) and edges represent thermal relationships. This allows queries like *"Show me all tasks that were hot last Tuesday and relate to Q3 marketing."*

## 📱 Responsive Interface

Built with a **mobile-first** mindset, every component gracefully degrades or enhances based on screen size:

- **Desktop (≥1024px)**: Full "Vulcan View" with multiple panes, thermal heatmaps, and drag-and-drop constellations.
- **Tablet (600–1023px)**: "Anvil Mode" — simplified sidebar, touch-optimized gestures, and adaptive font sizes.
- **Phone (<600px)**: "Spark Mode" — minimal UI showing only the most critical thermal metrics and a quick-add button. Swipe left to cool down tasks, swipe right to heat them up.

The responsive engine uses **container queries** (not just media queries), so even within a sidebar or embedded widget, the UI rearranges intelligently.

## 🔥 The Calor Engine

At the heart of Gryffin Forge lies the **Calor Engine**—a probabilistic algorithm that assigns each piece of content a *temperature value* from 0 (absolute zero/archived) to 100 (plasma/focused). The engine considers:

- Recency of interaction
- Frequency of access
- Emotional sentiment (inferred from word choice)
- Dependencies (a task blocking three others gets hotter)
- User-defined priority multipliers

The engine does **not** use cloud AI. All calculations happen locally via a pre-trained regression tree that updates with your behavior. You can even **export your Calor fingerprint** to transfer your thermal preferences to another device.

## 🔒 Security & Privacy

Your data remains yours. Always.

- **Zero-Knowledge Sync**: If you choose cloud sync, your data is encrypted with a key derived from your master password—Gryffin Forge never sees plaintext.
- **Local-First**: No account is required to use the software. Download, install, and forge away offline forever.
- **Audit Logs**: Every thermal event is logged locally so you can review how your data moves.
- **No Telemetry**: We do not track usage. The only "analytics" are your own thermal heatmaps.

## 🔌 API & Integrations

Gryffin Forge provides a **Local HTTP API** (opt-in) that runs on `localhost` for power users:

- Query thermal state, create nuggets, update temperatures
- WebSocket stream for real-time thermal changes
- Expose your thermal retrospective as a JSON snapshot
- Integrate with IFTTT, Home Assistant, or custom scripts

We also provide **native bridges** to common calendar systems (CalDAV) and email (IMAP via local proxy), all processed locally without sending credentials elsewhere.
